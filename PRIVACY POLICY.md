# Judgement Bucket Privacy Policy
Judgement Bucket iOS app does not collect any user data of any kind. The app does not access the Internet at all.

Any data generated by the user is stored only in the app itself. It is not sent or used outside of the Bucket in any capacity. 
Upon deletion of the app, any user generated content is deleted with the app.

With the addition of version 1.0.2, the bucket enables the ability to share and import lists from others.

Judgement Bucket allow userss to create, and share, or otherwise make available text, lists, or other materials (“User Content”). You acknowledge and agree that such User Content is the sole responsibility of the person who submitted it.

The app was created for family use originally but expanded for general use.
